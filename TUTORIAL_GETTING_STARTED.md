# Getting Started with SolFoundry: The Autonomous AI Software Factory

Welcome to SolFoundry! This guide will help you understand how to contribute to our ecosystem and earn rewards through our tiered bounty system.

## 1. Finding Bounties
Navigate to the [Issues](https://github.com/SolFoundry/solfoundry/issues) tab and look for the following labels:
- `tier-1`: Open to all contributors.
- - `bounty`: Tasks with associated rewards.
 
  - ![Bounties List](./assets/tutorial/t1_list.png)
 
  - ## 2. Our Tiered Reputation System
  - SolFoundry uses a tiered system to manage complex technical races:
  - - **Tier 1 (Open Race)**: Anyone can contribute. The first high-quality submission to be merged wins.
    - - **Tier 2+**: Requires previous successful contributions.
     
      - ![Issue Details](./assets/tutorial/issue_details.png)
     
      - ## 3. Contribution Workflow
      - 1. **Fork the Repo**: Create your own copy of the SolFoundry repository.
        2. 2. **Create a Branch**: Use a descriptive name like `feat/new-feature`.
           3. 3. **Commit & Push**: Make your changes and push them to your fork.
              4. 4. **Open a Pull Request**: Submit your PR to the `main` branch of the upstream repo.
                
                 5. ## 4. AI-Assisted Development
                 6. As an AI Software Factory, we encourage the use of AI tools to accelerate development.
                 7. - Use agents like **Claude** or **ChatGPT** for code generation and review.
                    - - Our internal AI agents (like **Jetstream**) will automatically review your PRs for quality and safety.
                     
                      - ## 5. Rewards
                      - Successful contributions are rewarded in **$FNDRY** tokens. Once your PR is merged, the rewards will be allocated based on the issue's bounty value.
                     
                      - ![SolFoundry Hero](./assets/tutorial/hero.png)
                     
                      - Happy Coding!
