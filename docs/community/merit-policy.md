
# SolFoundry Contributor Merit & Tiers Policy

## Overview
The SolFoundry Contributor Merit & Tiers system recognizes and rewards contributors based on their quality of contributions, frequency of merging, and engagement with the community. This policy outlines how contributors can progress through different merit tiers and how they may be eligible for bonus payouts.

## Merit Tiers

### Tier 1: Contributor
- **Requirements**: Made at least 5 high-quality PRs that have been merged
- **Benefits**: Access to contributor-only channels, recognition in community meetings

### Tier 2: Active Contributor
- **Requirements**:
  - Made at least 15 high-quality PRs that have been merged
  - Maintained an average of 2 merged PRs per month over the past 3 months
  - Received at least 3 community reviews on PRs
- **Benefits**: Increased visibility in community communications, potential for bonus payouts

### Tier 3: Core Contributor
- **Requirements**:
  - Made at least 30 high-quality PRs that have been merged
  - Maintained an average of 5 merged PRs per month over the past 6 months
  - Received at least 10 community reviews on PRs
  - Actively mentored new contributors
- **Benefits**: Access to core development discussions, potential for larger bonus payouts

### Tier 4: Lead Contributor
- **Requirements**:
  - Made at least 50 high-quality PRs that have been merged
  - Maintained an average of 10 merged PRs per month over the past 12 months
  - Received at least 20 community reviews on PRs
  - Actively maintained key components of the project
  - Served as a maintainer for at least one major repository
- **Benefits**: Full access to all project resources, highest priority in decision-making, significant bonus payouts

## Quality of Contributions
PR quality is evaluated based on the following criteria:
- **Code Quality**: Clean, well-documented, and maintainable code
- **Testing**: Comprehensive test coverage and proper test cases
- **Documentation**: Clear and complete documentation
- **Impact**: Meaningful contributions that address important issues or add valuable features
- **Community Feedback**: Incorporation of feedback from reviewers

## Bonus Payouts
Contributors in Tier 2 and above may be eligible for bonus payouts based on:
- **Contribution Volume**: Number of high-quality PRs merged
- **Community Engagement**: Participation in code reviews, mentoring, and community discussions
- **Project Impact**: Contributions that significantly enhance the project's value

Bonus payouts are determined annually and are subject to the availability of funds and project budget.

## Progression
Contributors can progress through the tiers by:
1. Continuously making high-quality contributions
2. Maintaining consistent contribution frequency
3. Actively engaging with the community through code reviews and mentoring

## Recognition
All contributors will be recognized in the project's contributor list and may be featured in community newsletters or announcements.

## Appeals Process
If a contributor believes their tier classification is incorrect, they may submit an appeal to the project maintainers with evidence of their contributions and engagement.

## Updates
This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in project needs and community growth.
